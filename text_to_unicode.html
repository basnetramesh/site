Typing rules & full mapping

How half-letters work: just type two consonants with no vowel between them — they auto-join with the halanta (्). e.g. chitthee → चिट्ठी, khwaappaa → ख्वाप्पा. To force a bare half-letter at the end of a word, add \ — e.g. ram\ → रम्.

Velar / palatal k क · kh ख · g ग · gh घ · ng ङ · ch च · chh छ · j ज · jh झ · yna ञ

Conjuncts ksh/kshy/x क्ष · gy/gny/jn ज्ञ · tr त्र
Signs * ं (anusvara) · ** ँ (chandrabindu) · : ः (visarga) · | । · || ॥ · OM ॐ

Tip: type in lowercase. CAPITALS are reserved for the retroflex (hard) consonants T Th D Dh N and Sh, so avoid capitalising the first letter of a word unless you want those.
